 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
This internship report outlines my experience in Data Analytics at Vivarttana Technologies. The 
primary objective of this internship was to apply data analytics techniques to analyse and predict 
sales trends using historical sales datasets and seasonal Diwali sales data. The project involved 
extensive data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and building 
 
Predictive models to optimize sales forecasting, marketing, and inventory strategies. 
 
Key activities included working with Python for data manipulation and machine learning tasks, using 
SQL for data extraction, and employing Power BI for data visualization. Additionally, I leveraged 
Google Colab for collaborative coding and model development, which facilitated efficient 
experimentation and model training. The primary models developed were linear regression, time 
series forecasting, and XGBoost, aimed at predicting future sales and identifying key drivers of sales 
performance. 
 
Throughout the internship, I also built interactive dashboards using Power BI, allowing stakeholders 
to explore and interpret the data, and tested various scenarios to predict outcomes based on historical 
trends. The insights from this internship can inform better decision-making in retail sales, helping 
businesses manage inventory and marketing strategies more effectively. 
 
This experience significantly enhanced my technical skills in Python, Power BI, Google Colab, and 
machine learning, while providing a solid understanding of the real-world applications of data 
analytics in the retail industry

Introduction 
Data Analytics plays a crucial role in understanding business operations, identifying trends, and 
making data-driven decisions. During my internship at Vivartana Technologies, I had the 
opportunity to dive into real-world data analysis by working on projects that involved sales 
forecasting, customer behavior analysis, and business optimization. The core objective of my 
internship was to apply data analytics techniques to analyze retail and seasonal sales data, build 
predictive models, and provide actionable insights to the business. 
 
Throughout the internship, I gained hands-on experience in using Power BI for visualizing large 
datasets and presenting data insights interactively. I also utilized Google Colab for coding, model 
development, and collaborative analysis, which enhanced my understanding of cloud-based 
platforms for data analytics. By using these tools, I was able to apply various machine learning 
models such as XGBoost, Linear Regression, and time series forecasting to predict sales patterns 
and identify key drivers of business performance.
